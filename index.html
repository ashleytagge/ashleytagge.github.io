Human-centered Threat Modeling

Motivation to use secure email

Interview study exploring mindsets behind adoption of secure email (e.g., ProtonMail). We found privacy motivations are strong, but encrypted email use remains rare—pointing to ecosystem and usability barriers. Read the paper ↗
